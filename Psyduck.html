About the Project

PsyAssist is a web + mobile platform that leverages AI personalities to guide, support, and connect users in their mental health journeys. With tests, reports, and anime-inspired companions, it makes psychological support engaging and accessible.

Key Features

Psychological Tests

Interactive tests with text, images, and video-based assessments.

Reports & Insights

Clear, graphical reports with recommendations and coping strategies.

Community Support

Groups built around shared AI personalities for peer support.

AI + Human Guidance

Seamlessly mix AI suggestions with volunteer/counselor help.

Multi-Personality Chatbots

Meet your anime-inspired companions, each with a unique personality to match your needs.

Serena (The Gentle Healer)

Soft-spoken and kind, always guiding you toward inner calm and peace.

Kai (The Motivator)

Energetic and fearless, cheering you on when life feels overwhelming.

Aiko (The Listener)

Empathetic and patient, she listens to your struggles without judgment.

Renji (The Analyst)

Smart and logical, helping you see patterns and practical solutions.
